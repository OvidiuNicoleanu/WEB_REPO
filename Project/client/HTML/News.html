News-SAG Awards 2022 Winners

Screen Actors Guild Awards (also known as SAG Awards) are accolades given by the Screen Actors Guild-American Federation of Television and Radio Artists (SAG-AFTRA). The award was founded in 1952 to recognize outstanding performances in movie and prime time television. SAG Awards have been one of the major awards events in the Hollywood film industry since 1995.

Actor's Stories

They all started with a dream...

There is a tradition of opening the Screen Actors Guild Awards, with a distinguished actor telling the audience something about their perceptions of their profession or a short biographical anecdote. Since the Inaugural Screen Actors Guild Awards, 107 actors have told their stories, typically closing with their name and the signature line, "I am an actor."

Our best dressed celebrities at 2022 SAG Awards

Lights, camera, action, and dresses! It was a glorious sight to behold on February 28th, 2022, as we saw many of our favourite celebrities walk the red carpet in stunning designer outfits at the Screen Actor Guild (SAG) awards. From Lady GaGa's showstopping white Armani Prive column gown with a sequin bodice to Cynthia Erivo's custom Louis Vuitton vinyl strapless gown, we can boldly agree that the red carpet was truly a magical moment.
